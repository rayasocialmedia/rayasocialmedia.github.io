  • About our internship program

Raya Social Media is offering an Internship Program. We are looking for graduating seniors who are original thinkers, curious, and creative with a passion for innovation and social interaction to join our team this summer. Our Internship Program provides a hands-on working/learning experience designed for college students interested in the field of social media, marketing and IT. Interns are required to work well in a fast‐paced work environment that will challenge their problem solving skills and be able to work independently, or in a team.

Qualifications

The ideal intern will possess strong communication skills, strong commitment to learning, and excellent computer skills, The Ideal Intern should be responsible, reliable and attentive to detail, should also be highly motivated, prompt, and organized

Benefits

Our Intern will gain valuable work experience, have an edge in the job market, decide if this is the right career path, have networking opportunities, apply classroom knowledge and gain confidence.
