About RSM

Emerging in the market with a strong presence as what is called a “Hybrid Agency” that offers a ONE STOP SOLUTION to what usually required multiple agencies to handle, Raya Social Media offers a full scope of digital services to include technical state-of-the-art Web & Mobile App development, UX consultation, design and implementation; as well as full Digital marketing services including Market Research, Competitive Analysis, Strategic Planning, Creative conceptualization & ideation, Social Media Management, Digital Advertising, Search Engine Optimization & Marketing and Digital Analysis. We take pride at being a team of dynamic, innovative and talented individuals eager to make a difference. We leverage on our experience with a diversified portfolio of clients to deliver effective solutions to businesses looking find an edge in the digital sphere.
