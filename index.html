About us

Raya Social Media, One of the newest subsidiaries of Raya Holding focused on enabling social interaction using online and mobile technologies. In Raya Social Media, we spread over dynamic innovation to find new interaction paradigms significantly enhancing and merging practices between the online and offline world to deliver positive impact on our daily life.

Projects

Connects you with people in your location who share your interests

Egypt’s first Social Network, OshareK brings together people who share common interests and allows them to interact on a comprehensive, collective and fun platform! OshareK is the one-stop hub for sharing, interacting and getting together based on mutual interest. It is a unique chance for users to engage with a wide-range of user-generated content relevant to their issues of concern and location.

It’s all about the neighborhood

Nassya is a unique experience that brings real neighborhoods to life in the online world! It is an exclusive platform where neighbors can interact more easily, explore new things in the neighborhood, and dig out true sense of belonging…a chance to revive lost community loyalty and aspiration… Nassya is real life seen with a fresh, inspiring and fun eye!

Empower the People... Trust the Government

Twasol E-Participation platform enables governments to pay attention to what their citizens have to say and respond to their requests, as well as involving citizens in the decision making process. It is a true dialogue between the governments and their citizens. Twasol is a way for both citizens and governments to co-operate; where citizens can see an added value and governments are empowering and including wide sectors of their societies.
