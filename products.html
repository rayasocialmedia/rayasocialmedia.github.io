Hatw5od

Spreading Knowledge

Hatwkhod.com is the first online library in Egypt that provides a unique book rental system to its subscribers. On Hatw5od library the subscriber can rent the most recent publications, and the best sellers, for small annual subscription fees. In a society that needs knowledge but can’t afford it, Hatw5od is the online solution to spreading knowledge at a low cost.


Tawasol

Empower the People... Trust the Government

Twasol E-Participation platform enables governments to pay attention to what their citizens have to say and respond to their requests, as well as involving citizens in the decision making process. It is a true dialogue between the governments and their citizens. Twasol is a way for both citizens and governments to co-operate; where citizens can see an added value and governments are empowering and including wide sectors of their societies.
